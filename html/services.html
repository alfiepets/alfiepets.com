About Our Service

Alfie strives to provide the perfect exercise experience for every pup on every visit - that means keeping your dog safe, secure, happy and healthy at all times. For that reason, we offer three distinctly different plans. We do realize, however, that some pups might need a different spin on one of our stock options, so we are more than happy to accommodate any adjustments to our services in order to help your pup reach maximum tail wag - all you have to do is ask!

What To Expect From Alfie

Top-Dog Customer Service

When we talk about customer service, we're not just talking about making you happy by being as responsive and affordable as any pet service provider in the industry. While we may pride ourselves on taking great care of you and your wallet, Alfie's primary focus is giving your furry family the exercise, attention and care that is required to live a long, happy and healthy doggy life!

Free In-Home Consultation

Think you're ready to become the newest satisfied member of the Alfie family? One of our pawsome pros will come to your home to meet you and your pup, assess his/her health and exercise needs, help you figure out exactly which plan is best for both of you, and answer any questions you may have! This is also a great opportunity for you to show us your pup's favorite walking spots, and to tell us all about those little quirks that make him so unique and lovable! Simply fill out a "Request a Free Consultation" form, or give us a call/email to get started!

Individual Attention

Alfie is not your average dog walker, and we're proud of that fact! Our plans are designed to give your pups the individual attention that they deserve. If you are the proud owner of multiple furbabies, we are certainly happy to walk them all together, and each of our professionals is experienced and comfortable with walking multiple dogs at once. At Alfie we care for your furry friends like they're our own, and you'll never see us potentially putting your dog in harm's way by walking him with dogs you don't know.

A Team You Can Trust

We realize that handing over a key to your home can be a stressful experience for some folks. That's why we take certain measures to make sure you can feel more comfortable with the Alfie team than anyone else out there. First off, all of our professionals are college-educated pet owners who have plenty of experience in dog walking and pet sitting their own dogs and others'. We also require that, prior to hiring, all Alfie team members be thoroughly screened through interviews, background checks and reference checks, and trained to the Alfie way of service that has made us so successful over the years. Second, Alfie is bonded and insured, meaning you can be confident that in the unlikely event an accident does happen, you, your home and your pup will be fully protected.
